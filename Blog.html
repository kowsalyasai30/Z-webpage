Houses

Posted on: 2024-02-20

A House rental agreement is required by law for both parties to have a valid contract. The agreement should include the property details, the owner, the tenant, the rental period, and the rent amount. It should be dated, signed, stamped, and registered.

Apartments

Posted on: 2024-02-15

An apartment is a residential unit that is part of one (or several) residential buildings, or a separate dwelling within a house with its own entrance, bathroom and kitchen. Apartments are typically one-story units within a multifamily or multi-unit building..
